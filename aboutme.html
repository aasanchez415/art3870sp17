I am Antonio Sanchez and I am 22. I was born and raised in San Francisco. I am currently working on a indie music label titled Flyzone Forever, and with that I am searching for my first hit that will allow me to have a chance to achieve a mainstream music career. My interest are in the arts and I have dedicated my life to it by shooting urban music videos that revolve around the edgy hard bass melodic sounds of the different parts San Francisco. In the midst of completing an undergrad at Cal state East-bay I’ve been working as an artist named “Itsfatfat”. I feel like threw life I use art to face objective and subjective parts of life which then allow me to create and express my reality.
